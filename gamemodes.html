Quick Play

Quick Play has two sub-modes. Quick Play - Classic is the classic game mode, where you are in an open queue for any game, and are able to choose any role and character. Quick Play - Role Queue is a game mode where you choose your specific role, before you queue up for a game.

Arcade

Arcade consists of different games that have different styles. This includes 1V1: Mystery Duel, 1v1 Limited Duel, 3v3 Elimination, 3v3 Lockout Elimination, 6v6 Lockout Elimination, 6v6 Mystery Heroes, 6v6 No Limits, Total Mayhem, Low Gravity, Capture the Flag, Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, etc.

Competitive

Competitive is a gamemode where communication is necessary because this game mode determines your ability to work with others under pressure and how you place among other players in the world.
